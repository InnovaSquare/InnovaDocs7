Android Subscription Agreement

1. Subscription Plans

Thank you for choosing our Android app. We offer the following subscription plans on Google Play:

  • Weekly Subscription: $2.99 per week (Auto-renewing)
  • Monthly Subscription: $4.99 per month (Auto-renewing)
  • Lifetime Subscription: $8.99 one-time payment (Non-renewing)

Note: Prices shown in USD. Local currency and applicable taxes may vary based on your Google Play account region.

2. Automatic Renewal & Billing

Important: All Weekly and Monthly subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled.

  • Payment will be charged to your Google Play account upon confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the same price ($2.99 for Weekly, $4.99 for Monthly).
  • You can manage and cancel your subscriptions at any time through your Google Play account settings or within the app.
  • Lifetime Subscription: This is a one-time purchase. It does not automatically renew and you will not be charged again.

3. How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription through any of the following methods:

  • In the app: Go to Settings → Manage Subscription (if available)
  • Via Google Play Store app: Open Play Store → Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Select this app → Cancel subscription
  • Via web: Visit https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions

For detailed instructions, see Google Play Help Center.

4. Free Trial (If Applicable)

If a free trial is offered:

  • Trial duration will be specified at signup (e.g., 3 days).
  • Unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid subscription.
  • You will be charged the applicable subscription fee ($2.99/week or $4.99/month) upon trial completion.
  • You may cancel the trial anytime during the trial period without being charged.

5. Refund Policy

Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will continue to have access to subscription features until the period ends. No refunds for partial periods, except where required by law.
